About DSU

Dayananda Sagar University, located in Bangalore, is a premier institution established under the Karnataka Act No. 18 of 2014. The university is built on the strong foundation of the 60-year legacy of Dayananda Sagar Institutions.

DSU offers innovative and career-oriented programs at the UG, PG, and Doctoral levels across Engineering, Management, Arts & Humanities, Law, Science & Technology, and Health Sciences.
